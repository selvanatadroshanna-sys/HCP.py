# Hotel Booking Analytics & Prediction

## Purpose

The Hotel Booking Management Assistant is an AI-powered business assistant designed to help hotel managers analyze booking performance, understand customer behavior, identify cancellation risks, and support operational decision-making.

The assistant combines booking analytics, cancellation prediction, and business intelligence into a single conversational interface.

---

## Business Problem

Hotel booking cancellations can lead to:

* Revenue loss
* Poor room allocation
* Inefficient staffing
* Inaccurate forecasting

The assistant helps management detect risks early and make proactive decisions to reduce cancellation impact.

---

## Available Data

The assistant can analyze information related to:

### Customer Information

* Country
* Customer Type
* Repeated Guest Status

### Reservation Information

* Lead Time
* Deposit Type
* Market Segment
* Distribution Channel
* Reserved Room Type
* Assigned Room Type

### Stay Information

* Arrival Date
* Number of Adults
* Number of Children
* Number of Babies
* Weekday and Weekend Stays

### Financial Information

* Average Daily Rate (ADR)

### Booking History

* Previous Cancellations
* Previous Successful Bookings
* Booking Changes
* Waiting List Information

---

## Assistant Capabilities

### 1. Booking Performance Analysis

The assistant can answer questions such as:

* What is the current cancellation rate?
* Which hotel has the highest cancellation rate?
* Which room types are booked most frequently?
* What are the busiest booking periods?
* How has cancellation behavior changed over time?

---

### 2. Customer Behavior Analysis

The assistant can identify patterns such as:

* Which customer type cancels the most?
* Are repeated guests more reliable?
* Which countries generate the highest cancellation rates?
* How do special requests affect booking outcomes?

---

### 3. Booking Channel Analysis

The assistant can evaluate:

* Market segment performance
* Distribution channel effectiveness
* Customer acquisition sources
* Cancellation rates by booking channel

---

### 4. Cancellation Risk Prediction

The assistant can evaluate a booking and determine whether it is likely to be canceled.

Example:

**Manager:**
Will this booking likely be canceled?

**Assistant:**
Based on booking characteristics and historical patterns, this reservation has a high cancellation risk.

---

### 5. Business Recommendations

The assistant can provide recommendations such as:

* Require deposits for high-risk bookings.
* Focus marketing efforts on reliable customer segments.
* Improve retention strategies for vulnerable bookings.
* Adjust pricing during high-cancellation periods.

---

### 6. Executive Decision Support

The assistant can help management answer questions like:

* Why are cancellations increasing?
* Which segment causes the greatest revenue loss?
* What operational risks should we monitor?
* What actions should management prioritize this month?
